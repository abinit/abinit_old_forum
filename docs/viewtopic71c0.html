Dear Abdullah,

The structure that one gets from your input file is not a correct one.
The Co atoms that are in the (x,y, 0) plane are too close to the atoms that in your structure are in the (x,y, 7.96024) plane ( Please see the figure with the overlapping spheres when the latter atoms are folded back in the primary unit cell due to the periodic boundary conditions(PBC) ).

Moreover Co contains in bulk 2 atoms in the primitive cell, so you could start for the monolayer with one atom in the unit cell with the appropriate lattice vectors, for example:

acell 4.737732368 4.737732368 15.0000 # the z direction should be large enough to eliminate the interaction between the single-layer and the replicas due to PBC. You will need to perform a convergence study for the total energy with respect to the size of the cell in the z-direction.

xcart
0.00000 0.000000 0.00000 #Bohr - Only one atom in the unit cell like, for example, in the case of graphene

Please let me know if you have any further questions.
Good luck!
