In principle yes. You'll probably have to do the following:

Code: Select all

../configure --enable-fc-wrapper --with-trio-flavor="none" --with-dft-flavor="none"

It is also possible that you have to provide some Fortran flags yourself to configure through FCFLAGS_EXTRA or FCFLAGS_OPTIM.
