Hi!

This may not be what you're asking for, but could be important.
By looking at your outputted xred's, it seems that none of your atoms have their coordinates fixed by symmetry. Therefore, you should not use iatfix and natfix in the optimization. If any of your atoms don't have fixed coordinates and you fix them in your input file (using iatfix and natfix), abitnit won't optimize them, and you will end up with unrelaxed structure.
