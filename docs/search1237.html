Re: Plane waves with Hartwigsen, Goedecker, Hutter psp??

PW are always the basis set in abinit. For PAW they are augmented by projectors in muffin tins.

You may _also_ use the gth/hgh potentials with the wavelet basis, though. The other psp do not work yet with wavelets.
