Hello,

what is wrong with the mode between M and K, and what do you mean by "previous" value at Gamma (you don't state if it's the LO or TO mode)?

The BS looks ok, so far. There are published versions in the literature, so you can compare. There are delicate effects due to Kohn anomalies and many body electron effects (see papers in PRB PRL by Francesco Mauri, Ludger Wirtz, and/or Claudio Attaccalite). These are beyond the calculation you are doing here...
