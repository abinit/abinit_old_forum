Re: band gap of silicon nanowire

Hi

Silicon is an insulator with a band gap of 1.12 eV and the density functional theory in its standard formulation is known to underestimate the value of the bandgap. If you want a good value for the band gap, you have to correct the density of states by using the GW approach for instance.
