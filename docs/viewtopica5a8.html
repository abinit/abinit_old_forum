The PAW+UPF files are not readable by abinit.
We support UPF1 for norm-conserving pseudos but PAW+UPF is more difficult to handle.
The internal implementation of the PAW formalism in Abinit differs from the one assumed in the PAW+UPF format and
it's still not clear if one can convert the UPF data into something that Abinit can use.

Note, however, that you can use the PAW JTH table available at

http://www.abinit.org/downloads/PAW2

to perform PAW calculations with/without SOC in Abinit and in all the other PAW codes that support the PAW-XML specifications
