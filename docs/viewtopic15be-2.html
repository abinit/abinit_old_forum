Posted: Tue Aug 30, 2011 4:45 pm
by proffess
Postdoctoral Position (Research Associate) Announcement:

Two postdoctoral positions are available in the group of Prof. Inna Ponomareva (http://shell.cas.usf.edu/~iponomar/index.html) at the University of South Florida, USA. The successful candidates will work on the first-principles and first-principles-based simulations of ferroelectric and multiferroic materials (in their bulk and nanostructure forms).

Required qualifications: (i) PhD in Physics or related field; (ii) strong research accomplishments in condensed matter/quantum chemistry and a demonstrated record of research productivity; (iii) excellent computer programming skills; (iv) background/experience with high-performance computing; and (v) good communication skills.

Applicants should send (by email in PDF format as a single attachment):
i) a curiiculum vitae,
ii) list of publications, description of research interests and
iii) names, phone numbers, and e-mail addresses of at least three references to:

Prof. Inna Ponomareva, Department of Physics, University of South Florida, Tampa, FL 33620; e-mail: iponomar@usf.edu

See http://shell.cas.usf.edu/~iponomar/index.html for more information.
