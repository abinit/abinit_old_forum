Post by jzwanzig » Mon Dec 20, 2010 12:09 pm

Here's an input file for actual wurtzite (ZnS) to get you started.

ecut 15
pawecutdg 30

prtgeo 2

toldff 1.0D-6

acell 3.8227 3.8227 6.2607 angstrom
angdeg 90. 90. 120.
spgroup 186

kptopt 1
kptrlen 60

ntypat 2
znucl 30 16

natom 4
natrd 2
typat 1 2

xred
1/3 2/3 0
1/3 2/3 0.3748

nstep 50
iscf 17
diemac 2.4
