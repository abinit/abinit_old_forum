Two points:

1) Do DOS calculation in serial mode. I got wrong results previously using parallel mode, and if you browse through the forum you can also find people reporting about inconsistencies in its intensity.

2) Your fermi energy is -0.03430 Hartree. In your graph above did you shift your fermi energy to zero? From visual inspection, the DOS at fermi energy level is indeed zero.
