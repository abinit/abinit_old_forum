Re: Install Abinit on Redhat HPC [SOLVED]

Your MPI library does not support MPI_INTEGER16.
To make abinit compile:

1) Open the config.h file in your editor and remove the line:
#define FC_INT_QUAD 1

2) make clean
3) make -j4
