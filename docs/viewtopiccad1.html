Nope, no explicit anharmonic calculations yet, maybe someday soon. This can be done in quantum espresso, for gamma point phonons only.

What you _can_ do is look at the main contribution which is thermal expansion: do phonons at 2 volumes and get the Gruneisen parameters as the finite difference derivative of the phonon frequencies wrt volume (there are several posts on this in the forum, I believe).
