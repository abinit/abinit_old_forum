This is probably not acoustic sum rule breaking, for once, so either the phase is genuinely unstable (ok, that won't really be the case) or you are not converged wrt some parameter...

Try doing just the soft phonon with even higher ecut/nkpt/nband. Btw, have you looked at the phonon scf convergence, to make sure it was ok? Which pseudopotentials are you using?
