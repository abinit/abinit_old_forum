Can't you just add it by hand? I mean, given that H = U + pV, where U is the internal energy of the unit cell (etotal in the abinit output), p is your target pressure, and V the unit cell volume (ucvol in abinit output), can't you just calculate the pV contribution? (be sure to use consistent units, ucvol is in atomic units, therefore Bohr^3).
