M.Sc. and Postdoctoral fellowships in Thunder Bay (Canada)

Posted: Wed Aug 18, 2010 7:31 pm
by orubel
Currently, one Postdoctoral and two Graduate Student fellowships are available in order to support ongoing development of novel semiconductors for solid-state detectors in medical imaging at Thunder Bay Regional Research Institute (Canada). Atomic-scale simulations (incl. first-principle calculations ABINIT, Wien2k, VASP) are intended to facilitate improvement of critical properties of materials for imaging detectors by gaining understanding of fundamental physical mechanisms responsible for degradation of detector's performance.

Interested individuals are encouraged to check details at http://www.tbrri.com/~orubel/jobs.html
