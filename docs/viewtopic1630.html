Dear abgs01,
I'm not sure of what you want to do exactly, but to me you should leave abinit calculate the symmetry operation automatically in all your cases. Because you are using non-primitive cells and chkprim=0, abinit does not print the space group but you can check it with Abinit or qAgate that use spglib or you can use a crystallo software suite (like ISOTROPY suite) to verify the space group of your system.
