Dear Zhang,

Razvan Caracas published a few papers on Raman spectra calculated with abinit. You can, for example, use the procedure from one of his papers in order to obtain the spectrum like in the figure you sent:

Physics of the Earth and Planetary Interiors 174 (2009) 113–121

It seems to me that the Raman line widths (FWHMs) were taken from the experimental data. If you'd like to calculate them, you could follow the procedure used by Cardona and his team, for example in:

PRL 90, 055510 (2003)

I hope this helps.
