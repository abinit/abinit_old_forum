Posted: Fri Apr 15, 2011 10:06 pm

Dear ABINIT users,

At the request of one of our important funding sources, a user survey
is organized in the context of the European Theoretical Spectroscopy Facility (http://www.etsf.eu).
This survey targets several first-principles applications,
forming the so-called "ETSF software suite", that includes : ABINIT, APE, DP, Elk, Exc, Exciting,
FHI98PP, Octopus, V_Sim, YAMBO, see e.g. the ETSF pages
http://www.etsf.eu/resources/software/codes
http://www.etsf.eu/resources/software/etsf_software_repository

Your opinion about this service is valuable. Would you be so kind to show
your involvement by giving a two minutes (at most) of your time to fill the short form at http://www.etsf.eu/node/363,
before April 20 ?

Thanks in advance,

Your code developers.
