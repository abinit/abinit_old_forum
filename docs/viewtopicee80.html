Re: 6x6x6 Monkhorst-Pack in spinels

Posted: Thu Aug 23, 2012 4:27 pm
by jzwanzig
Your third option, kptrlatt 6 6 -6
-6 6 -6
-6 6 6
would be coherent with an FCC cell. If you're ever in doubt you should do a run with

kptopt 1
prtkpt 1
kptrlen 60 (or some other suitably large value)

and abinit will print out choices for kptrlatt and shiftk that are consistent with your input structure.
