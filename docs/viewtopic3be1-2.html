For a good overview on this topic and many others, see the book by RM Martin, Electronic Structure, Cambridge. Basically ngkpt together wtih nshiftk and shiftk are selecting the parameters of a Monkhorst-Pack grid of points in k space. Then, based on the value of kptopt, symmetry is used to reduce this set. The result is the number (nkpt) of k points actually used in the calculation, the effect of the symmetry related points that are not used explicitly is included through the effective weight of each point, which is determined internally.
