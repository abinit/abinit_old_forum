I'm interested in how anaddb deals with imaginary phonon frequencies to calculate the phonon free energy because
\[
F_vib = k_BT int_0^{\infty} ln {2 sinh [\frac {\hbar \omega}{2k_B T}]} g (\omega) d \omega
\]
