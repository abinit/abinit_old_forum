- Max_threads: 1
- Num_threads: 1
- Num_procs: 1

- Nested: F

runjob --np 256 --ranks-per-node 4 --envs "OMP_NUM_THREADS=8" : abinit < calc.files > calc.log

Am I missing something obvious? Thanks in advance for your help.
