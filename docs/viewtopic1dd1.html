Registration Abinit User-developer interaction June 1 and 4, 2021

Posted: Sat May 01, 2021 5:22 pm
by gonze
Dear ABINIT users,

One of the key points of ABINIT v9 is the improvement of the user experience (documentation, launch, complete pseudos tables, â€¦).
In order to continue this effort, and linked to the 2021 ABINIT developer workshop, we will host two online "users meet developers" sessions of direct live discussion via videoconference, on specific topics chosen by users.
The sessions are scheduled on :
- Tuesday 1st June 10:00-12:00 CEST (most convenient for Europe and Asia users and developers e.g. 16:00-18:00 in Beijing and 17:00-19:00 in Tokyo)
- Friday 4 June 20:00-22:00 CEST (most convenient for American users and developers, e.g. 14:00-16:00 New-York/Montreal and 11:00-13:00 California).
Virtual discussion rooms will be open and offer ABINIT users the possibility to meet developers and discuss problems, challenges and requests.
In each room with a general topic, several developers will be present.
The discussions will be prepared and oriented according to the needs of the participants.

Registration is now open, see the page
https://www.abinit.org/developersmeetusers .

The organisers
M. Torrent, B. Amadon, F. Jollet, M. Verstraete, and X. Gonze
All times are UTC+02:00
