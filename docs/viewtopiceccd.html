Re: pseudopotentials for Gw calculations

by bruneval
GW pseudopotentials should include semicore states. For Indium, I'd advise to have one including 4s, 4p and 4d as valence.
I guess the one you propose is not suitable.
Concerning oxygen, no problem.

Fabien

Re: pseudopotentials for Gw calculations

Posted: Wed Apr 14, 2010 12:25 pm
by bruneval
For sure, the HGH pseudopotential is not compatible with the current implementation of the KSS generator.

You should take a look at the header of the pseudopotential for Indium and discover how many electrons they consider as valence. 3 ? 13 ? or 21 ?
For proper GW calculation, I'd bet that you need a 21-electron pseudo.

Fabien
