EMRS fall 2018 Symposium I

Posted: Tue May 01, 2018 6:52 pm
by antonio
We would like to encourage abstract contribution for talks at the EMRS Fall 2018 Meeting - Symposium I: "Atomic-scale design protocols towards energy, electronic, catalysis and sensing applications". The Symposium will take place in Warsaw, Poland, from September 17 to September 20.

The Symposium is open to researchers from all materials science branches (physics, chemistry, engineering, materials informatics, etc), focusing on investigations towards materials design for energy harvesting/storage, sensing, catalysis, magnetic and optoelectronic applications.

Hot topics covered by Symposium I are:

Design of 2D and 3D materials
Layered heterostructures and interfaces
Quantitative prediction of structural response to external stimuli
Catalytic, sensing, electronic, photonic and optoelectronic applications
Instrumentation and analysis technique development (hardware, software)

Confirmed Speakers:

Pablo Ordejon, Institut Català de Nanociència i Nanotecnologia, Spain
