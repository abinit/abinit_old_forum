Let us denote the xred of original cell by "x y z". Then the supercell will be:
x/2 y/2 z/2
x/2+0.5 y/2 z/2
x/2 y/2+0.5 z/2
x/2+0.5 y/2+0.5 z/2
x/2 y/2 z/2+0.5
x/2+0.5 y/2 z/2+0.5
x/2 y/2+0.5 z/2+0.5
x/2+0.5 y/2+0.5 z/2+0.5
