As discussed at the CECAM tutorial in Lausanne with you, a few weeks ago, this was indeed a bug...
ABINIT versions before 2006 worked just fine for ZrO2 , but a bug had been introduced then.
This bug has been fixed in ABINITv6.0.4, available today. I hope you will be able to correctly
reproduce the phonon band structure of ZrO2 that was published a long time ago.
Thanks for your careful testing, and reporting.
