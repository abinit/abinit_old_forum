This error should not appear, and I don't know whether it comes from your system or Abinit.

To work this around, just copy /path/to/abinit/fallbacks/transient/lapack-abinit_6.0.tar.gz into ~/.abinit/tarballs/.

If your machine has no direct access to the internet, you'll have to copy all tarballs manually from http://www.abinit.org/downloads/plug-in-sources.
