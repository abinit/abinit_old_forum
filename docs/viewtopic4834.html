Hi,

The difference between the two formulations resides in the computation of the exchange-correlation terms. In the Bloechl formulation, the exchange-correlation potential does not include the compensation charge density whereas in the Kresse formulation, the potential includes the compensation charge density. You can choose between the two formulations thanks to the input variable usexcnhat (see http://www.abinit.org/documentation/helpfiles/for-v6.2/input_variables/varpaw.html#usexcnhat).

David
