Post by jzwanzig » Mon May 27, 2013 1:11 pm

you might try setting kptopt to 4 instead of 1, that will use spatial symmetries only but not time reversal. Also, I note you are allowing for pretty large overlap of the PAW spheres, that's not good, it may significantly degrade your accuracy. You need to use PAW spheres with smaller cutoff radii.
